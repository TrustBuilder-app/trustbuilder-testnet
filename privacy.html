Privacy Policy

TrustBuilder respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data.

  • Authentication: We use Pi Network’s SDK to authenticate users. No passwords or personal data are stored.
  • Reviews: Submitted reviews are tied to your Pi username and stored securely in our database.
  • Payments: All Pi payments are processed via the Pi SDK and routed to the app wallet. We do not store payment credentials.
  • Data Usage: Review data may be used to generate trust scores or tier access. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Security: All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS and stored in a protected Supabase instance.
  • Contact: For questions or data requests, email trustbuilderapp@gmail.com.

By using TrustBuilder, you agree to this privacy policy.
